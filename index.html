About Indian Independence Day

Indian Independence Day is celebrated on August 15 each year. It marks the end of British rule in India in 1947 and the establishment of a free and independent Indian nation.

Historical Timeline

  • 1857: First War of Indian Independence.
  • 1942: Quit India Movement.
  • 1947: India gains independence from British rule.
  • 1950: Adoption of the Indian Constitution.
